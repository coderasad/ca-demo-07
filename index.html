Why Choose TiCash

Most Haitians receive remittances from family members abroad to run their households. Where once the process of picking up the remittance was filled with large bank deductions and not being able to receive the funds in the nearest financial portals available, TiCash makes it easy to instantly receive money in your MonCash or bank account.


Users can send and receive as little as $5 if they need to do so and be discreet about it as well. Very little time is spent between a user sending the money and an individual in Haiti being able to use the funds.


The TiCash app works in collaboration with MonCash which has outlets all over Haiti. This makes it easier for users to receive funds who have had to travel long distances to financial institutions with their accounts.

Superior User Experience

The TiCash app is designed to focus on navigability for all users. With a clean interface, the app can cater to a satisfying customer experience.
